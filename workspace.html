Workspace management

Freedom & Governance best of both worlds

Start the trial
Your gains with Parta.io

  • All your team at one place

    Parta.io is built as a team workspace. Forget the frictions of individual accounts. Now your team can easily share the courses, branding themes, media library and custom templates.

  • Enhanced Security & Control

    Granular access control lets you define user roles and permissions for each workspace and individual course ensuring data is only accessible to dedicated people.

  • Clear multi-team / 3rd party management

    Multi-workspace architecture lets you create dedicated workspaces for different parts of your company or invite your clients/partners to collaborate with them safely.
Benefit from operating as a team

User roles & permissions

Define permission to each team member ensuring everyone has the needed access while maintaining data security. Grant control at both the workspace and individual course level for ultimate flexibility.

Project (or content) library

Build your own content library organized your way. The whole team can access and use existing materials to create courses faster!

Media library & file storage

Ensure everyone has access to the latest media assets and files by managing your central “Resources” library. The most up-to-date media assets allow to be all the creators on the same page.

Template collections

Empower your team with a library of pre-designed templates. Share layouts and ready-made content blocks, like legal disclaimers to streamline course creation and leverage less experienced team members.

Branding library

Design and store unique themes for each brand or unit within your company. Everyone creates consistently branded courses and spend time on what matters.

Collaboration between workspaces

Multi-Workspace Architecture

Create separate workspaces for each team if needed. Each workspace provides complete control over roles and permissions ensuring organized collaboration and project management.

Cross-workspace Sharing

Courses: Seamlessly share courses across company workspaces using unique IDs.

Branding Themes: Export and import custom branding themes between workspaces. Ensure a cohesive learning experience for your audience, even with multi-company collaboration.

3rd-party Collaboration

Robust permission management functionality & multiple workspace architecture allow you to develop efficient & secure collaboration with 3rd parties (e.g. contractors, clients, partners)
