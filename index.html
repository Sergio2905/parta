Manifesto

The eLearning content creation market has been dominated by a few vendors for too long. Stagnant innovation, outdated architecture, constant price increases, and slow update cycles have stifled progress. It's time to democratize eLearning development and breathe new life into it.

Our vision is to introduce a Figma/Miro-like experience to the L&D world, breaking down silos and enabling seamless collaboration between L&D and non-L&D professionals.

Accelerate eLearning creation

Easy collaboration between L&D and non-L&D worlds allows for the decentralization of eLearning creation and enables broader teams.

Break down silos

Enjoy the architecture of team workspace collaboration. Manage workflows, ensuring freedom without compromising governance.

Empower instructional designers

Simplify the day-to-day routines of instructional designers with multiple productivity improvements, while fostering creativity opportunities.

We are democratizing eLearning creation without compromising quality.

Realtime collaboration

Co-create in real time

Create eLearning together in real time, streamline the review process with element-level feedback, and navigate the history of changes.
Templates & no-code editor

Enhance pre-built templates with no-code editor

Quickly develop courses with pre-built templates, or create custom layouts of any complexity at no-code editor.

P.S. Don't forget to share new templates with your team so that you can create much faster!

Branding

Streamline your (re)branding process

Ensure consistent branding across all course elements, down to the finest detail. Create multiple themes and share them for easy application, making (re)branding a matter of just a few clicks, even for dozens of courses!
Task management

Benefit from end-to-end task management

Assign tasks and set deadlines during project planning or editing. Tasks are automatically organized on a Kanban board enabling you to track progress and seamlessly navigate to specific content blocks.

Break down silos and bring your team together

Customize roles and access with Parta.io's permission management system and enable collaboration across different teams with our multi-workspace architecture. Streamline workflows by seamlessly sharing everything with your team, even across workspaces. Templates, branding, entire projects – whatever.
