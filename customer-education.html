Explore Parta.io integrations

Connect your integrations, Close your tabs

We understand how much needs to be embedded into your courses and how crucial it is to eliminate unnecessary friction and extra steps when developing and updating content. That’s why Parta.io integrates seamlessly with your favourite apps and platforms, streamlining your workflows and enhancing efficiency.

  1. Seamlessly embed your media content from video hosting or digital asset management platforms.
  2. Localize your content seamlessly by integrating with your TMS or CAT integrations, taking advantage of translation memory and in-context translation updates.
  3. Publish and update courses directly to your LMS without the hassle of downloading and uploading SCORM files.
