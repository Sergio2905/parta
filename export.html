Your gains with Parta.io

  • Universal compatibility

    Reach every learner, regardless of platform. Export courses in SCORM, PDF, HTML formats for seamless integration with any LMS or offline access.

  • Public links to any part of your course

    Share content easily by creating and sending public links to a full course, a section, or even a content block.

  • Robust export settings&tracking

    Deeply customize SCORM export settings and benefit from automatic version recognition when re-uploading courses to your LMS. Easily access all your export history.

Tailored exports

Exclude course sections or content blocks to customize your exports for different audiences. Hidden content won't appear in SCORM packages, HTML, or public links.

Export to LMS with Custom IDs

Update your course without disrupting ongoing learners. Choose "Custom" and add your LMS course ID. Re-upload prompts the LMS admin to update or keep the current version.

Export history

Never lose sight of your exports. Parta.io logs all your export history for easy access.
