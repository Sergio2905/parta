Advanced custom layouts

Build unique templates with any layout imaginable. Create templates with as many columns as you need. Want a video background for a quiz? You got it!

Experiments with interactive elements

Combine elements in unique ways – imagine Tabs hiding an accordion with different types of elements inside... The possibilities are endless.

Advanced settings for elements

Customize the placement, size, padding, margins, and visual appearance of each element. Control everything from color schemes and fonts to borders and sizes to match your course aesthetics and brand guidelines.

Love a layout? Save it as a template for future courses! Share templates with your team to streamline the design process and let them focus on content.
